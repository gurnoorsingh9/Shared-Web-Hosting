About Us

G Host is a modern, student-friendly web hosting platform designed to simplify the process of deploying static websites. Our mission is to make web hosting accessible to everyone, especially students and developers who are just starting their journey.

What We Offer

  • 💾 100 MB Free Storage - Perfect for learning projects and portfolios
  • 🌐 Custom Subdomains - Get your own site.domain.com address
  • 📁 File Management - Easy upload, delete, and organize your files
  • ☁️ Instant Deployment - Your site goes live immediately after upload
  • 🔒 Secure Hosting - HTTPS enabled for all sites

Perfect For

  • 🎓 Students learning web development
  • 💼 Portfolio websites
  • 📝 Personal blogs and projects
  • 🚀 Testing and development
